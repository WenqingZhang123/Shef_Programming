Welcome to Kinda Cloudy.

Kinda Cloudy is my latest stab at web development. It's not the most original, but I like the clean feel it displays. It's not too image heavy either, so I like that. There's only one bug that I've come to notice - the second row of gallery thumbnails doesn't sync up with the first in internet explorer.

That's what you get for using internet explorer, I suppose.
